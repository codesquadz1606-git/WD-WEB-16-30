What is Table in HTML?
Table consists of rows & columns.
Where we display data in tabular format.

table tag 
    tr tag (table row)
        td/th(table data / table head)

td & th : Both are used to define the column. but the diffrence is th is in bold & center align, whereas td is in normal form.

: border = "Adds the border"
: cellspacing = Space b/w outer cell & inner cell

:cellpadding = Space b/w cell & content

Column Merge : Colspan (horizontal merge)
Row Merge : Rowspan (vertical merge)
