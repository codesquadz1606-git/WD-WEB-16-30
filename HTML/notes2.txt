What is List?
List are of two types:
1. Ordered List 
    Ordered list is denoted by ol tag 
    Ol tag has 5 types : 1,I,i,A,a
    OL tag is a parent tag & whereas li tag is the child tag

2. Unordered List
    Unordered list is dnoted by ul tag
    UL tag has three types : square, circle, disc
    UL tag is a parent tag & whereas li tag is the child tag

What is Linking?
It means linking a url or a internal page.
a tag helps us to link a page.
a tag stands for anchor tag.

to open any link on a new tab we will use target attribute and _blank value.
